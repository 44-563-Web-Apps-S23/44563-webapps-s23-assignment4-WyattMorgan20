Wyatt Morgan Snips a Play

The Last Straw

By: Bosworth Crocker

BAUER.

I can't eat nothin'. I'm full up to here.

[_He touches his throat._

MRS. BAUER.

If you haven't done nothin' wrong, why do you let it worry you so?

[_Children are absorbed in eating._

FRITZI.

[_Suddenly._] Gee, didn't Blacky like liver!

[MRS. BAUER _and_ KARL _look at him warningly_.
